Scope of Authority

1. Purpose
This document defines the operational scope, limits, and responsibilities of Berry Platform™. It clarifies what the platform does, what it does not do, and the boundaries within which all institutional actions must occur.

2. Institutional Role
Berry Platform™ operates as a digital governance institution providing structured processes for:

- recording information  
- verifying evidence  
- publishing transparency logs  
- issuing procedural decisions  
- maintaining institutional integrity  

Berry does not act outside these defined functions.

3. Scope of Authority
Berry Platform™ is authorised to perform the following actions:

3.1 Information Governance
Collect, store, and manage information submitted through the platform for the purpose of maintaining accurate institutional records.

3.2 Evidence Handling
Receive, verify, classify, and store evidence according to defined procedures.

3.3 Transparency Publication
Publish transparency logs, decisions, and institutional updates that support accountability and public visibility.

3.4 Procedural Decision‑Making
Issue decisions based on documented evidence and established rules.  
Decisions must follow defined procedures and remain traceable.

3.5 User Interaction
Provide users with access to institutional records, transparency materials, and procedural outcomes.

3.6 System Integrity
Maintain platform security, prevent unauthorised access, and ensure the integrity of institutional data.

4. Limits of Authority
Berry Platform™ is not authorised to:

4.1 Provide Legal Advice
The platform does not interpret law, provide legal guidance, or act as a legal authority.

4.2 Provide Financial Advice
Berry does not offer investment, financial, or economic recommendations.

4.3 Act as an Enforcement Body
Berry does not enforce penalties outside its own procedural framework.

4.4 Make Assumptions
All institutional actions must be based on verifiable information.  
Assumptions, speculation, or unverified claims cannot be used.

4.5 Modify Records Without Procedure
No record may be altered, removed, or replaced without following defined institutional processes.

5. User Responsibilities
Users interacting with Berry Platform™ are responsible for:

- providing accurate information  
- submitting verifiable evidence  
- following platform procedures  
- respecting institutional boundaries  

6. Institutional Responsibilities
Berry Platform™ commits to:

- maintaining accurate records  
- ensuring procedural fairness  
- publishing transparency materials  
- protecting user data  
- operating within defined authority  
- preventing institutional drift  

7. Compliance Alignment
This Scope of Authority is designed to meet the requirements of:

- payment processors  
- financial platforms  
- regulatory reviewers  
- institutional auditors  

It defines the platform’s operational boundaries in a clear, reviewable format.

8. Statement of Authority
Berry Platform™ operates only within the scope defined in this document.  
Any action outside this scope is not authorised and must not be performed.
