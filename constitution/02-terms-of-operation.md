Terms of Operation

1. Purpose
These Terms of Operation define how Berry Platform™ functions, how users interact with the system, and the responsibilities of both the institution and its users. This document provides a clear operational framework for platform use.

2. Platform Function
Berry Platform™ provides structured digital governance services, including:

- information recording  
- evidence handling  
- transparency publication  
- procedural decision‑making  
- access to institutional records  

The platform operates within the boundaries defined in the Scope of Authority.

3. User Access
Users may access Berry Platform™ to:

- submit information  
- provide evidence  
- view transparency logs  
- receive procedural outcomes  
- interact with institutional processes  

Access may be restricted if a user violates platform procedures or security requirements.

4. User Responsibilities
Users must:

- provide accurate and truthful information  
- follow platform procedures  
- respect institutional boundaries  
- maintain the security of their own accounts  
- comply with all applicable laws  

Users are responsible for any information they submit.

5. Institutional Responsibilities
Berry Platform™ commits to:

- maintaining accurate records  
- ensuring procedural fairness  
- protecting user data  
- publishing transparency materials  
- operating within defined authority  
- maintaining system integrity  

The platform will not act outside its authorised scope.

6. Prohibited Use
Users may not:

- submit false or misleading information  
- attempt to access restricted areas  
- interfere with platform operations  
- misuse institutional processes  
- engage in unlawful activity through the platform  

Violations may result in restricted access or removal of submissions.

7. Data Handling
Berry Platform™ stores information for the purpose of maintaining institutional records.  
Data is handled according to:

- procedural requirements  
- transparency obligations  
- security protocols  

The platform does not sell or distribute user data.

8. Decision Framework
Procedural decisions are:

- based on documented evidence  
- issued through defined processes  
- recorded for transparency  
- subject to oversight  

Decisions are not influenced by assumptions or speculation.

9. Amendments
These Terms of Operation may be updated to reflect:

- procedural improvements  
- security requirements  
- transparency obligations  
- institutional growth  

Updates will be published through the transparency layer.

10. Acceptance
By using Berry Platform™, users agree to these Terms of Operation and acknowledge the platform’s defined scope and procedures.
