4.0 Asset Layer Overview

Purpose of the Asset Layer

The Asset Layer defines the constitutional rules that govern the existence, identity, truth, and survival of every asset within the Berry Platform™.It is the foundation upon which all worlds, systems, and integrations rely to ensure that digital reality remains coherent, verifiable, and reconstructable.

The Asset Layer is not a technical spec.It is a constitutional guarantee that assets cannot drift, mutate silently, or lose their history.

What the Asset Layer Protects

The Asset Layer ensures:

Identity is immutable and cryptographically anchored.

Provenance is complete, continuous, and tamper‑evident.

Lineage is reconstructable across worlds and timelines.

Integrity is verifiable at any moment.

Custody is transparent and auditable.

Mutation is controlled, logged, and reversible.

Reconstruction is always possible, even after catastrophic loss.

Resurrection is constitutional, not ad‑hoc.

Deprecation preserves truth rather than erasing it.

Arbitration resolves conflicts without rewriting history.

Compliance is representational, never destructive.

Security is zero‑trust and enforced at every layer.

Disaster recovery is guaranteed by design.

Interoperability preserves canonical truth across external systems.

Redaction is constitutional, not editorial.

Stress testing ensures assets survive extreme conditions.

This layer is the bedrock of Berry’s digital truth model.

Digital assets fail in traditional systems because:

identity is mutable
