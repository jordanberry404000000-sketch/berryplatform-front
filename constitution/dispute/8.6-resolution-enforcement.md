8.6.1 Purpose

Resolution Enforcement defines how Berry carries out the outcomes of disputes and appeals.This chapter ensures that:

resolutions are executed consistently

resolutions cannot be ignored or delayed

enforcement cannot be bypassed

actors and worlds comply with final decisions

institutional fairness becomes institutional action

A resolution is not complete until it is enforced.

8.6.2 Enforcement Authority

Resolution enforcement may be carried out by:

Local Enforcement

For minor corrections and actor‑level adjustments.

System Enforcement

For cross‑world changes, authority updates, or structural corrections.

Governance Enforcement

For constitutional rulings, precedent updates, and high‑severity reversals.

Enforcement authority must match the scope of the resolution.

8.6.3 Enforcement Requirements

Every enforcement action must include:

resolution identifier

enforcement action type

actor or world affected

authority snapshot

evidence summary

timestamps

enforcement signatures

world‑context

impact assessment

Enforcement must be deterministic and auditable.

8.6.4 Enforcement Actions

Resolution enforcement may require:

1. State Correction

Updating chain state, lineage, or custody outcomes.

2. Authority Adjustment

Granting, modifying, or revoking authority.

3. Sanction Adjustment

Increasing, decreasing, or removing sanctions.

4. Procedural Correction

Re‑running processes that were performed incorrectly.

5. Governance Implementation

Applying constitutional interpretations or precedent.

6. Institutional Correction

Updating rules, procedures, or system logic.

Enforcement actions must match the resolution exactly.

8.6.5 Enforcement Timing

Enforcement must occur:

immediately for critical resolutions

within constitutional time limits for all others

without delay, suppression, or obstruction

If enforcement is delayed, the system must escalate automatically.

8.6.6 Enforcement Logging

Every enforcement action must be:

logged immutably

hash‑linked

cryptographically signed

globally consistent

accessible to the appropriate transparency tier

If enforcement is not logged, it is invalid.

8.6.7 Enforcement Failure Modes

Berry must detect and prevent:

partial enforcement

selective enforcement

forged enforcement actions

enforcement suppression

enforcement tampering

contradictory enforcement

enforcement loops

Any interference with enforcement is a constitutional violation.

8.6.8 Constitutional Principle

A decision without enforcement is noise.Enforcement without fairness is tyranny.Berry guarantees both.
