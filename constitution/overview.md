Berry Platform™ Constitution Overview

Purpose of the Constitution

The Berry Constitution establishes the foundational rules, principles, and governance structures that define how digital truth is created, preserved, validated, and protected across all worlds, timelines, and systems operating within the Berry Platform™.

It is not a whitepaper.It is not a technical spec.It is not a manifesto.

It is a constitutional framework — a living, enforceable, zero‑trust governance model for digital reality.

Why a Constitution Exists

Digital systems fail when:

Constitutional Principles

The Constitution is built on five core principles:

Truth is immutable.

Governance is explicit.

Security is zero‑trust.

Compliance is representational, not destructive.

Worlds are sovereign but canonically aligned.

Structure of the Constitution

The Constitution is organized into layers:

Asset Layer (4.x) — identity, truth, integrity, and survival

World Layer (5.x) — world rules, sovereignty, and governance

Multiverse Layer (6.x) — cross‑world coherence and arbitration

Operational Layer (7.x) — platform operations and enforcement
