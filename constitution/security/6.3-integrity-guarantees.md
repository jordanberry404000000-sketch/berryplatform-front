6.3 Integrity Guarantees

6.3.1 Purpose

The Integrity Guarantees define the cryptographic, structural, and operational requirements that ensure all Berry Platform™ data remains authentic, tamper‑evident, and verifiable.These guarantees protect canonical truth from corruption, manipulation, and adversarial interference.

Integrity is not a property.Integrity is a constitutional contract.

6.3.2 Integrity Domains

Berry enforces integrity across six domains:

Identity Integrity

Provenance Integrity

Lineage Integrity

Custody Integrity

Metadata Integrity

World‑Context Integrity

Each domain is independently verifiable and cryptographically anchored.

6.3.3 Identity Integrity

Identity integrity ensures that:

identityHash is immutable

identity signatures are valid

identity cannot be spoofed

identity cannot be reassigned

identity cannot be overwritten

Identity is the root of trust — attackers always try to break the root.

Berry guarantees:

deterministic identity verification

non‑repudiable identity signatures

cryptographic binding between identity and all actions

If identity integrity fails, all dependent actions must be rejected.

6.3.4 Provenance Integrity

Provenance integrity ensures that:

all provenance events are signed

events are ordered and tamper‑evident

no event can be inserted, removed, or reordered

provenance chains remain continuous

provenance signatures remain valid

Berry guarantees:

hash‑chained provenance logs

signature verification for every event

continuity checks for every chain

If provenance integrity fails, the asset is considered compromised.

6.3.5 Lineage Integrity

Lineage integrity ensures that:

parent‑child relationships are cryptographically linked

lineage cannot be collapsed or rewritten

lineage chains remain continuous

lineage mutations are impossible without signatures

lineage history is immutable

Berry guarantees:

deterministic lineage reconstruction

cryptographic parent‑child binding

tamper‑evident lineage transitions

If lineage integrity fails, canonical truth cannot be trusted.

6.3.6 Custody Integrity

Custody integrity ensures that:

custody transitions are signed

custody cannot be fabricated

custody cannot be reassigned without authority

custody chains remain continuous

custody history is non‑repudiable

Berry guarantees:

signature‑anchored custody events

custody chain verification

enforcement of custody authority

If custody integrity fails, the asset must be quarantined.

6.3.7 Metadata Integrity

Metadata integrity ensures that:

all metadata mutations are authorized

mutations are signed and logged

unauthorized mutations are rejected

metadata cannot override canonical truth

metadata cannot be injected by hostile worlds

Berry guarantees:

mutation signature verification

schema‑level validation

world‑context isolation

If metadata integrity fails, the mutation must be rejected.

6.3.8 World‑Context Integrity

World‑context integrity ensures that:

world data cannot override canonical truth

world‑context events are isolated

hostile worlds cannot corrupt assets

world‑context must be validated before ingestion

world‑context cannot fabricate provenance or lineage

Berry guarantees:

strict world isolation

world‑context verification

adversarial world handling

If world‑context integrity fails, the world must be treated as hostile.

6.3.9 Integrity Enforcement

Integrity guarantees are enforced through:

signature verification

hash‑chain validation

continuity checks

canonical reconciliation

deterministic verification logic

enforcement escalation

Integrity enforcement is mandatory and cannot be bypassed.

6.3.10 Integrity Violations

Any attempt to:

forge signatures

break hash‑chains

collapse lineage

fabricate custody

inject unauthorized metadata

override canonical truth

…constitutes an integrity violation.

Integrity violations must trigger:

immediate rejection

logging

zero‑trust enforcement

governance escalation

Integrity violations are treated as attempts to corrupt truth.

6.3.11 Constitutional Principle

Integrity is the backbone of truth.Truth is the backbone of the platform.Compromise neither.
