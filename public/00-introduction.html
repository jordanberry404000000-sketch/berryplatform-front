00 — Introduction

The Berry Platform Constitution establishes the foundational principles that govern the creation, preservation, verification, and restoration of digital truth across all Berry systems.

Berry is not a product or protocol — it is a digital governance institution. Its purpose is to ensure that identity, provenance, lineage, and integrity are not optional features but constitutional guarantees.

This introduction outlines the philosophical and structural basis upon which the rest of the Constitution is built, defining Berry’s role as a steward of digital truth and a protector of institutional integrity.
