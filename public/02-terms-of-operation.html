02 — Terms of Operation

The Terms of Operation define how Berry functions as a constitutional platform, including its operational rules, guarantees, and obligations.

These terms ensure that:

  • Truth cannot be rewritten
  • History cannot be forged
  • Creators cannot be erased
  • Assets cannot lose their provenance
  • Governance remains transparent and accountable

The Terms of Operation establish the operational integrity of Berry and ensure that all systems remain aligned with the founding mission of protecting digital truth.
