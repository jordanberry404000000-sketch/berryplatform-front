01 — Scope of Authority

The Scope of Authority defines the powers, responsibilities, and limitations of the Berry Platform as a digital governance institution.

Berry’s authority extends to:

  • Identity verification and provenance tracking
  • Truth preservation and historical integrity
  • Governance of digital assets and their lineage
  • Enforcement of constitutional guarantees
  • Oversight of platform operations and compliance

Berry’s authority is strictly bounded by constitutional principles. It cannot exceed its mandate, alter truth, or compromise the integrity of digital history.
