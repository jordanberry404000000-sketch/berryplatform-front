Asset Layer

The Asset Layer defines how digital assets are registered, verified, tracked, and preserved within the Berry Platform. It ensures that every asset maintains a provable lineage and cannot be altered, erased, or forged.

The Asset Layer guarantees:

  • Immutable provenance for all registered assets
  • Transparent ownership and transfer history
  • Protection against tampering or falsification
  • Permanent linkage between creators and their works
  • Institutional-grade auditability

This layer forms the foundation of Berry’s truth-preserving architecture, ensuring that digital assets remain trustworthy across their entire lifespan.
