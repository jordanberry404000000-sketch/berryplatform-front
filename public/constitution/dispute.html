Dispute Resolution Layer

The Dispute Resolution Layer defines how conflicts, challenges, and disagreements are resolved within the Berry Platform.

It provides:

  • Procedures for submitting disputes
  • Neutral review and evaluation processes
  • Institutional responsibilities for resolution
  • Transparent outcomes and documentation
  • Protection against retaliation or misuse

This layer ensures that disputes are handled fairly, transparently, and in alignment with Berry’s constitutional mission.
