Enforcement Layer

The Enforcement Layer defines how constitutional guarantees are upheld and how violations are addressed within the Berry Platform.

It establishes:

  • Mechanisms for enforcing truth and provenance
  • Procedures for addressing violations
  • Institutional responsibilities for enforcement
  • Safeguards against misuse of authority
  • Public accountability for enforcement actions

This layer ensures that Berry’s constitutional principles are not symbolic — they are actively protected.
