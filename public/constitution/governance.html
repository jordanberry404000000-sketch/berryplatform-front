Governance Layer

The Governance Layer defines the decision-making structures, institutional roles, and constitutional processes that guide the operation of the Berry Platform.

It establishes:

  • Governance authorities and their responsibilities
  • Decision-making procedures
  • Checks and balances
  • Oversight mechanisms
  • Constitutional amendment processes

This layer ensures that Berry operates with transparency, accountability, and institutional integrity.
