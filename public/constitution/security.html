Security Layer

The Security Layer defines the protections that safeguard Berry’s systems, users, assets, and governance processes from threats, manipulation, or compromise.

It ensures:

  • Integrity of platform operations
  • Protection against unauthorized access
  • Resilience against attacks or tampering
  • Secure identity and provenance verification
  • Institutional-grade cryptographic safeguards

This layer ensures that Berry remains a trusted environment for digital truth.
