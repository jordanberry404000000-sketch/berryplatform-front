Transparency Layer

The Transparency Layer ensures that Berry’s operations, governance decisions, and institutional processes remain visible, auditable, and accountable to the public.

It guarantees:

  • Public access to governance records
  • Visibility into platform operations
  • Clear documentation of decisions and amendments
  • Open audit trails for assets and identity
  • Institutional accountability

This layer ensures that Berry remains a public institution, not a black box.
