Constitution Overview

The Berry Platform Constitution establishes the foundational rules, guarantees, and operational principles that govern digital truth, provenance, identity, and institutional integrity across all Berry systems.

This overview outlines the structure of the constitutional framework, including:

  • The purpose and mission of Berry as a digital governance institution
  • The scope of authority granted to the platform
  • The operational terms under which Berry functions
  • The governance layers that ensure transparency, security, and accountability
  • The enforcement and dispute mechanisms that protect truth and provenance

The Constitution is a living document, versioned and maintained to ensure that Berry remains aligned with its founding mission: to protect digital truth as a public good.
