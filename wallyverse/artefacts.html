What This Page Is

The Artefact Index is the official registry of all canonical items within the Wallyverse. Every artefact listed here is registered through the Creator Console and backed by provenance, creator identity, and version history.

These artefacts shape the universe — they hold meaning, power, history, and narrative weight.
