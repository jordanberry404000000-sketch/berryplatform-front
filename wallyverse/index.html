What is Wallyverse?

Wallyverse is a living creative universe — characters, stories, worlds, and artefacts — all anchored to Berry’s institutional guarantees of identity, provenance, and integrity.

Every piece of Wallyverse content can be traced, verified, and restored. Nothing is “just a file” — it’s part of a provable lineage.

Enter Wallyverse

Core Pillars

Characters & Lore

Persistent characters with verifiable histories, relationships, and canonical timelines — protected against erasure or silent rewrites.

Worlds & Timelines

Worlds that evolve over time, with every major event recorded as part of an immutable narrative ledger.

Artefacts & Editions

Scenes, covers, editions, and artefacts registered as assets on Berry’s Asset Layer, with full provenance and creator lineage.

Provenance by Design

Wallyverse is not “content on a chain” — it is a universe whose existence depends on institutional guarantees of truth.

Creator Lineage

Every artefact is cryptographically linked to its creator, collaborators, and institutional context.

Canonical vs. Non‑Canonical

Canon is explicitly marked, versioned, and preserved. Experiments and alternates are still traceable — but never confused with the core.

Restoration & Recovery

If platforms fail or files disappear, Wallyverse can be reconstructed from Berry’s institutional record.
