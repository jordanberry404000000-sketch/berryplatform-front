How the Timeline Works

The Wallyverse timeline is not linear — it is a branching, living structure shaped by canonical events, narrative fractures, and provenance‑verified lore.

This map outlines the major timeline layers and how they relate to each other.

1. Baseline Timeline

The Baseline

The Split

Universe‑defining event • Provenance ID: EVT‑SPLIT‑001
